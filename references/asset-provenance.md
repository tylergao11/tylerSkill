# Asset Provenance

Use this when creating, importing, reviewing, or shipping art, audio, video,
fonts, generated images, or third-party assets.

## Asset Provenance Record

```markdown
## Asset Provenance Record

Asset:
Source: Generated | User Provided | Purchased | Third Party | Internal
Creator or Vendor:
License:
Commercial Use Allowed: Yes | No | Unknown
Modification Allowed: Yes | No | Unknown
Attribution Required:
Restrictions:
Evidence:
Decision:
```

Do not ship assets with unknown commercial rights without explicit user approval
and recorded risk.

Generated assets should record the prompt/source context when useful for future
style consistency or rights review.
